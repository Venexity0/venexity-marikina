History of Marikina

Marikina has a rich history, from its early beginnings to becoming the shoe capital of the Philippines. One of its notable urban legends is the "White Lady" who is said to roam around the city.

Riverbanks Tiangge in November

Every November, the Marikina Riverbanks transform into a bustling tiangge, showcasing local crafts, food, and festivities.
